About Us

Learn more about Eligo Recruitment and our mission to transform the recruitment industry.

Who We Are

Eligo Recruitment initially started as a candidate placement agency and as the years went by, we realized clients approaching us to help them fill various roles which has now become our core operations.

We aim at ensuring both parties are content i.e. client and candidate. We match candidates with organizations where we feel they will grow as individuals and help the company grow as well.

We have been in the industry for about 5 years now and have done successful placements having worked with numerous clients within the private and public sector.
