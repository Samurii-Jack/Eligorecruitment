Who We Are

Eligo Recruitment began as a candidate placement agency, evolving into a comprehensive recruitment solutions provider. Today, we're proud to serve organizations across the private and public sectors, helping them build exceptional teams.

Our mission is simple: ensure mutual satisfaction. We match candidates with organizations where they'll flourish as individuals while driving company growth. Every placement we make is a partnership built on understanding, trust, and shared success.

With a proven track record of successful placements and a deep understanding of the African talent landscape, we're uniquely positioned to meet your recruitment needs with precision and care.
